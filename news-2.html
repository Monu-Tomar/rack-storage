Pulished on Nov. 13, 2024

Airport fences

Space-saving design for efficient organization.

Usually made of high-strength, corrosion-resistant materials, such as welded steel mesh, Y-shaped protective columns, and accordion-type barbed wire. The design has anti-climbing and anti-impact functions to ensure the safe isolation of the airport area.The appearance is beautiful and coordinated with the airport environment.

Application scenarios

Isolation and protection of key areas such as airport runways, aprons, and terminals. Traffic isolation and safety warnings on roads around airports.

Main application groups

Airport management department: responsible for the planning, construction and maintenance of airport fences. Security personnel: responsible for patrolling and monitoring the fenced area to ensure airport safety.!

Application scenarios

Isolation and protection of key areas such as airport runways, aprons, and terminals. Traffic isolation and safety warnings on roads around airports.

Types of fences that can be used at airports

Reinforced mesh fence: a high-security isolation and protection net made of welded steel bars with good permeability and safe isolation effect. Airport fence: a fence specially used for airport isolation, with the characteristics of stability, firmness, beauty and elegance.
