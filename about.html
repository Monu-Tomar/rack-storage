Jasiva Building Material Technology Co., Ltd.

Established in 2018, Jasiva is committed to quality manufacturing and excellent customer service on high-tech wire mesh and related metal products.

We are located in Shanghai, China. Just 25 kilometers away from Anping, the hometown of wire mesh. Our factory covers an area of 15000 sqm, we are a production/R&D enterprise.

After 30 years of innovation-driven development, we have become China′s leading manufacturer and exporter of wire mesh & metal products.

We produce and export various types of storage cages, garden planters, laser cut decorative panels, guardrails, blade barbed wire, and horticultural metal products. We have the most advanced automatic welding equipment, laser cutting equipment, and plastic spray production lines.

Our products are widely used in subways, factories, industrial sites, real estate, residential quarters, government schools, stadiums, municipal roads, airports, ports, landscaping, and more.

The company has a sound scientific management system and a strong technical force team. Our products are exported to Europe, America, Canada, and other countries, with rich export experience and a perfect after-sales service system.
