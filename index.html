How many years can your product last?

What is the normal surface treatment?

(a) Galvanized then silver color paint the welding point.
(b) Galvanized raw material, after welding then go for painting(PVC power coated 0.1mm).
(c) Raw material is no galvanized then hot dipped galvanized after welding.
